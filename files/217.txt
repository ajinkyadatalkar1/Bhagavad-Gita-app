(9) Sirs, when the person who was killed by Shrikrishna in a fit of anger had also become worthy of
realization of Brahman, how can Arjuna to whom he had given the teaching with great love not
realise the absolute Brahman?
(10) When we become one with Krishna, Krishna becomes our own consciousness and naturally
occult powers come of their own accord to meet our desire.
(11) But only Arjuna was having that extreme love, and therefore his aspirations were always
fulfilled.
